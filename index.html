Give your clients the control they want with an online customer ordering portal

The wholesale customer ordering portal gives your customers the freedom they want to place their own ad-hoc orders, manage their own order templates, apply for credit, and make payments on account. Combined with the RCS customer loyalty and promotions modules, up selling is automated removing the need for you to pick up the phone!

Other benefits include:

  • Reduce order processing costs
  • Seamless integration between online portal and the RCS ERP software
  • Sales prompts via email, text messages and apps
  • All actions (customer enquiries, complaint management etc) alerted in the RCS workflow system
  • Take control of your production facilities

    Our production control services make it easy to monitor and efficiently run all of your facilities (e.g. bakery and butchery):

    • Touch screen production recording
    • Touch screen stock control (e.g. on shop floor or cutting plant)
    • Trace stock through the system (e.g. batch control, carcass control)
    • Automated labelling based on recording stock
    • Integrated weighing scales

  • Easily manage distribution and supply remotely

    Our distribution services allow you to efficiently utilise a number of replenishment methods, including the apportionment of stock across multiple orders, automated replenishment, drop shipping to customers, and spreadsheet, website API and other third-party integrations.

    RCS mobile devices allow for the smooth implementation of crucial services such as picking, stock control, receipts and labelling, purchasing and ordering, and more!
